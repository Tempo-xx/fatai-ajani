The Challenge

A home goods brand was spending $3,500/month on Meta ads with a disappointing 1.8× ROAS — well below break-even. Campaigns were broad, ad creative was stale, and there was zero retargeting infrastructure. A full restructure was needed.

Strategy & Execution

  • Rebuilt campaign architecture using a 3-tier funnel — cold (TOF), warm retarget (MOF), cart abandoners (BOF)
  • Developed 8 new ad variants — UGC-style video, carousel, and static formats
  • Launched TikTok Ads targeting 18–34 demographic; allocated 20% of budget to test and scaled winners
  • Deployed Google Shopping campaigns for branded and high-intent product search terms
  • Configured all Pixel events — ViewContent, AddToCart, Checkout, Purchase — enabling algorithmic optimisation
  • Built lookalike audiences from top 5% purchasers to expand reach while maintaining acquisition quality

The Challenge

A beauty brand with a 12,000-subscriber email list was generating less than 6% of revenue from email — far below the industry benchmark of 25–35%. No automated flows existed, campaigns were irregular, and the list had never been segmented.

What Was Built

  • Designed 5-email Welcome Series — brand story, bestsellers, first purchase incentive, social proof, values
  • Built 3-step Abandoned Cart flow: reminder → social proof → urgency offer, recovering 18% of lost carts
  • Created Browse Abandonment and Post-Purchase flows nurturing customers toward repeat buys
  • Developed Win-Back sequence for customers inactive 60–90 days, recovering 12% of lapsed segment
  • Implemented advanced segmentation — VIPs, one-time buyers, at-risk cohorts, high-LTV customers
  • Rebuilt all campaign templates for mobile-first design, improving CTR by 22%
