The Challenge

A home goods brand was spending $3,500/month on Meta ads with a 1.8× ROAS — well below break-even. Campaigns were broad, creative was stale, and there was no retargeting infrastructure. A full campaign restructure was required.

Strategy & Execution

  • Rebuilt campaign architecture using a 3-tier funnel — cold (TOF), warm retarget (MOF), cart abandoners + high-intent (BOF)
  • Developed 8 new ad variants — UGC-style video, carousel, and static formats with new creative briefs
  • Launched TikTok Ads targeting 18–34 demographic; allocated 20% of budget to testing and scaled winners
  • Deployed Google Shopping campaigns for branded and high-intent product search terms
  • Configured all Pixel events correctly — ViewContent, AddToCart, Checkout, Purchase — enabling algorithmic optimisation
  • Built lookalike audiences from the top 5% of purchasers to expand reach while maintaining quality

The Challenge

A beauty brand with a 12,000-subscriber email list was generating less than 6% of revenue from email — far below the industry benchmark of 25–35%. No automated flows existed, campaigns were irregular, and the list had never been segmented.

What Was Built

  • 5-email Welcome Series — brand story, bestsellers, first purchase incentive, social proof, brand values
  • 3-step Abandoned Cart flow: soft reminder → social proof → urgency offer, recovering 18% of abandoned carts
  • Browse Abandonment and Post-Purchase flows nurturing customers toward repeat buys
  • Win-Back sequence for customers inactive 60–90 days, recovering 12% of the lapsed segment
  • Advanced segmentation by purchase history, LTV, and engagement — VIPs, one-time buyers, at-risk cohorts
  • Full campaign template redesign for mobile-first rendering, improving CTR by 22%

How I work

The engagement process

Every engagement follows the same four-stage framework — diagnosis before action, measurement throughout, and compounding improvement over time.

01

Audit & Diagnose

Full 360° audit across traffic, funnel, UX, ads, email, and analytics — surfacing exactly where revenue is being lost and why.

02

Prioritise by Impact

Findings ranked by impact-to-effort ratio. The highest-leverage fixes go first — so results come in weeks, not months.

03

Execute & Test

Every change is implemented with A/B testing discipline. Nothing is assumed — winning variants are scaled, losers are cut quickly.

04

Report & Compound

Clear weekly reporting with business context — not just numbers. Each cycle builds on the last, compounding gains across the full lifecycle.
